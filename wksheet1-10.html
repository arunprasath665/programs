Tirupati is a city in Chittoor district of the India state of Andhra Pradesh.The city is home to most famous and important vasihnavite shrine of Trimula Venkateswara Temple
and other historic temples and is referred to as Spiritual Capital of Andhra Pradesh.

        The temples was built and upgraded in parts by various kingdoms.The inscription in the temple were found inKannada,Sankrits,Tamil,Telugu languages which spcify the
contribution of the Pallava kingdom around the 9th century AD.
